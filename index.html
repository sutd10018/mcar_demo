How to use this demo

STEP 1

Open the "Lecturer's view" link in this page on a desktop/laptop browser.

This mimics the lecturer/teacher's PC in the classrooms.

Change/type the math equations in the GUI and click "Display Graph" to update the 3D surface.

Click the "Push to AR" button in GUI to push the current equation to students' AR view.

STEP 2

A page displaying the QR code is displayed on screen.

This mimics the projector screen display that students see in the class.

STEP 3

Acting as a student/viewer, scan the QR code on the screen with your mobile phone.

You will be directed to a webpage where you will need to confirm the equation before proceeding to AR view.

NOTE: this step is only a temp solution since we are not using web server.

STEP 4

Allow camera access when prompted by your browser.

You can modify the parameters in the equations and see the 3D surface updated in real time.

NOTE: (known issue, to debug) the 3D surface cannot be rotated while on AR/camera mode.
